anthem is dystopian fiction novella by russian american writer ayn rand written in and first published in in the united kingdom
the story takes place at an unspecified future date when mankind has entered another dark age technological advancement is now carefully planned and the concept of individuality has been eliminated
a young man known as equality rebels by doing secret scientific research when his activity is discovered he flees into the wilderness with the girl he loves
together they plan to establish new society based on rediscovered individualism rand originally conceived of the story as play then decided to write for magazine publication
at her agent suggestion she submitted it to book publishers the novella was first published by cassell in england it was published in the united states only after rand next novel the fountainhead became best seller
